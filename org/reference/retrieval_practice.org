#+title: Retrieval Practice

The act of retrieval (or recall) reinforces memories. This causes us to forget
knowledge more slowly. If we strategically space out retrieval ([[id:55090bb9-dc8a-4c92-97fb-6617cf8d37b4][Spaced
Repetition]]), then we can effectively halt forgetting.
